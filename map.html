Restrictions (important - please read!)

Confetti Please do not bring your own confetti as the venue has strict policies regarding what type can be used. We'll be making sure there's plenty to go around 😊

Alcohol Please do not bring your own alcoholic beverages to the venue. All drinks must be purchased on-site (there's a large safety deposit which we'll lose if any of our guests break this rule). There's a lovely bar with a wide variety of drinks available.

Taxis
