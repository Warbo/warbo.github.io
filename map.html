Restrictions (important - please read!)

Confetti Please do not bring your own confetti as the venue has strict policies regarding what type can be used. We'll be bringing the confetti to you!

Alcohol Please do not bring your own alcoholic beverages to the venue. All drinks consumed on-site must be purchased onsite (we're not kidding - there's a hefty safety deposit which we'll lose if any of our guests break this rule).

Taxis

We strongly recommend pre-booking your taxi back home/back to your accommodation in advance. We'll be providing some local taxi firm numbers closer to the time. Our venue has advised that there have been cases when guests have taken the chance and not pre-booked and have had to wait in the cold for 30+ mins.
